Young Scientists Programme

In our Young Scientists Programme, we provide world-class educational ecosystems aligned with the highest global standards in cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Robotics, Drone Technology, 3D Printing, Sensors and more.

Primary (7–10 Yrs)

The programme is meant to provide hands-on experience of block-based coding, robotics, and STEM activities to young children under expert guidance.

Intermediate (11–14 Yrs)

The programme is meant to cultivate the brilliance of young scientists in the fields of robotics, drones, AI, IoT and sensors while building teamwork and creativity.

Advanced (15–18 Yrs)

The programme is designed for advanced learners with programming, robotics, machine learning, data science and real-world projects.

We believe children are the future and the present. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where children grow, learn and innovate.
